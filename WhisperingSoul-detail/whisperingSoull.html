Lately, everything feels heavy. You're tired, but sleep doesn't help. You take breaks, but never feel rested. You keep showing up, but it's like you're watching life happen from the outside.

That's not just stress. That's burnout.

Burnout isn't always dramatic. Sometimes it's quiet:

  • You lose interest in what you used to love.
  • You go numb during conversations.
  • You feel guilty for slowing down, even when you're running on empty.

Rest might pause the exhaustion. But recovery is what heals it.

And recovery looks different. It's messy and slow. It's saying no without guilt. It's letting go of unrealistic standards. It's remembering that your worth isn't tied to your productivity.
